Snatch 
Power Snatch + Squat Snatch + OHS, 2 min
1-1-1-1-1
The complex today is mainly going to be limited off of the power snatch.  Key focus should be on foot position when receiving the barbell.  It should be consistent and steady so you don’t have to move those feet at all for the OHS.  This should be your guide on what weight you should use.  Adjust accordingly throughout your work sets and if you need to drop the bar between the first 2 movement to work on the pull off the floor that’s fine.
Metcon (Time)
3 Rounds
20 Back Squat (185/135)
10/6 Ring Muscles Ups
Goal: Sub 14 min
RX+- 10 Ring Muscle Ups
Red - 155/105, C2B pull ups us a band if needed
White - 95/65, pull ups with band or ring rows

If you are comfortable and experienced with it you can take the barbell from the floor.  Otherwise make sure to have it come from a rack for safety reasons.  Weight should be something you can do unbroken for at least the first set if not the first 2 sets.  Steady and methodical reps to keep moving here.
