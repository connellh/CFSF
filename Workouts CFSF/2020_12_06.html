Box Squat 
Every :90 x 10 Rounds:
3 Box Squat

- Stay within the range of 70-80%.
- Box set at parallel.
- Hips back, vertical shins.
Bench Press 
Every :90 x 10 Rounds:
3 Bench Press

- Stay within the 70-80% range.
- Grip should be a thumb length outside of chest.
- Keep shoulders pinched to the bench with a smooth, controlled descent.
