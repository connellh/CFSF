Primal ALTERCATION Partner WOD

Metcon (AMRAP - Reps)
Both partners complete exercises in order.
1 partner works, other rests.
During rest minute perform 3 burpees to plate.

2 Rounds - partners alt work/rest
1: mtn climbers
2: pull-ups
3: kb snatch
4: v-ups
5: kb swings
6: wall balls
7: DUs
8: 300M Run - start together on min.

Total time to complete 1 complete rd is 16 min. WOD time is 32 min.

Scale movements for YOUR ability.

Score total reps combined.

**ADV option: 4 burpee box jump overs on rest minute.
