Metcon (Time)
20.5 Sweatin' to the Oldies Partner WOD
For time, partitioned any way and split the work as needed with your partner:
40 ring muscle-ups
80-cal. row
120 wall-ball shots, 20-lb. ball to 10 ft/14-lb. ball to 9 ft

Time cap: 20 min.
For those that have RMU, you are expected to do them.  If you do not have RMU, your modified option will be BURPEE CHEST 2 BAR.

Teams that have 1 partner that can do RMU but the other can't, the partner that can do the RMU is expected to do so while the other will help out with burpee C2B to split the 40 reps as needed.

Good luck to everyone!
