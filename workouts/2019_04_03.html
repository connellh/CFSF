Push Jerk 
Push Jerk, 2 min
4 x 5 reps

*Cycling these can be tough so work on keeping that chin back and elbows in front of your wrists when brining the bar back to the front rack. You want that bar to continually find the shelf of your shoulders and not rest in your wrists which is no bueno.  Adjust weight across each set.  Speed under the bar is going to be key here so focus on that throughout.
