Happy Thursday! Today is a recovery day, so this workout is for those who want a little extra! The purpose is to just move. Grab a partner and split the reps however you want. Have some fun!

AMRAP 16 Minutes:
100 Jumping Jacks
100 Air Squats
100 Push Ups
100 Alternating Leg V-Ups (Total)
100 Alternating Step Back Lunges (Total)
100 Burpees
Individual version - cut the reps in half
