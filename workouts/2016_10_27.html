Metcon (AMRAP - Reps)
"Rowling"

With a partner, each will row 10 frames of 200m.  The goal of each frame is to get the computer to stop on exactly 200m.  The partner closest to 200m is the winner, with the loser performing 1 burpee over the rower for each meter over/under he or she is.

*Score will be how many total burpees you perform.
