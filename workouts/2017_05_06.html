5/6 Partner WOD

Metcon (Time)
2 Rounds

---Partner 1: Run 400M
---P2:  15 Burpee Slam Balls (25lb+/15lb+)
*Alt. and repeat.

after each had ran/ burpee combo then
---P1+P2: 10 Partner DLs 275/185
---Rest 60  seconds and repeat above 1x time

-THEN rest 60 seconds again and...

2 Rounds

---Partner 1:  500M Row
---P2: 25 Pushups
*Alt. and repeat.

after each had ran/ burpee combo then
---P1+P2: 10 Partner DLs 275/185
---Rest 60  seconds and repeat above 1x time

NOTES: Partners work at same time in
Row/Pushups and Run/Burpees

Partners lift together, same bar same time on deadlifts

Time cap 25 min, scale meters to 400M if need, run to 300M, pushups to 20, burpees to 10, and weights accordingly.
