Bench Press 
On a 3-minute clock, for 4 sets:
CLOSE GRIP Bench x8 @ 70-75%

*Perform this with a shoulder-width grip.  If normal benching grip is already this narrow, keep it the same.

**Between each set, begin setting up for WOD.  Snatch progressions and build up sets to working weight.
Metcon (AMRAP - Rounds and Reps)
GG Workout 5
AMRAP 14:
70 DU's
7 Muscle-Ups
7 Snatches (155/105)

RED- 7 pullups/7 pushups per MU, 115/85# Snatch
WHITE- 70 singles, 7 pullups, 7 situps, 7/7 DUMBELL snatches

***You can perform any style of snatch, but it is recommended that you power snatch all of these reps, so please choose a weight that allows you to do so.
