Metcon (AMRAP - Reps)
Four AMRAPs: 8, 6, 4, and 2 min, with 2 min rest between each.

8 med ball sit-ups (20/12)
8 med ball squats (20/12)
8 cal row
8 pull-ups
8 push-ups

For each AMRAP after the first, the last exercise drops off the list. (The 6-min AMRAP is just the first 4 movements,  etc.)
