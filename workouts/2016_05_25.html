Deadlift 
In 10 Minutes, build to a heavy double Deadlift.  Use the following rep scheme:

5-3-3-2-2-2...

RX and RED- Heavy Double
WHITE- 5RM

** Perform the heaviest double possible with good technique and controlling the descent.  All reps must be performed as touch & go and the bar must be controlled down to the floor between reps 1 and 2.
