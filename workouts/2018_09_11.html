Metcon (AMRAP - Rounds and Reps)
AMRAP 30 min
Row 1k
50 Thruster (75/55)
25 Bar Muscle Up
50 Box Jump Over (24/20)
Bike 100 Cal
Goal: 2 Rounds
Red & White - see notes below

Long one today so pace it well. The goal should be right about 2 rounds so scale down the reps if needed.  Loading is light so work for bigger sets here and reasonable breaks between them.  If you don’t have bar muscle ups yet try a band or jumping option today since you can work higher reps.  If you do have them and are working to develop them scale down the reps and see if you can get 10.  If you need to sub out a bike feel free toss in a 1 mile run.  Aside from being smart with the bar mu and not going to failure this one is all about just "going".
