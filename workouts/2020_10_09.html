Metcon (2 Rounds for time)
2 Sets (2nd set starts at 10:00)
50/40 calorie echo bike
*Every :90 do 3 Squat Snatches (70% of 1RM)
*Start the workout with Squat Snatches
Squat Snatches: This should not be over 70% 1RM and you should be able to hit 3 reps with good form under fatigue in under 30 seconds each time you go back to the bar.

Echo Bike: We need to be fast here as we will only get about 60-70 seconds on the bike at a time. Keep pace fast but be ready to get right back on the bar every :90

The SCALING aim is to use appropriate loading relative to your 1 RM and have a HIGH intensity each set!
