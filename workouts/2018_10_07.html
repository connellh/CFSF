Metcon (AMRAP - Rounds)
Do one movement every 30 seconds for 20 minutes.

1-2 Squat Clean(s) (see below)
4 Burpees to Plate
6 T2B
8 KBS (55/35)

Each full round will take 2 minutes. You'll do 10 rounds total.
- For squat cleans, you can choose to do 1 rep at 80%, or 2 touch-and-go reps at 50%.
- You can switch squat clean rep schemes in the middle of the workout. At any time, you're free to add weight and drop to 1 rep, or drop weight and move to 2 reps
- Do not work past 20 seconds for any movement
- Keep your gear grouped tight. You will not have much time to transition.
