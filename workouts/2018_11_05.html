Metcon (Time)
For time:
15-12-9-6-3
Power Snatch (115/75)
2 for 1 Wall Ball (20/14)
Goal: Sub 8 min/ Cap 10 min
Red - 95/65
White - 75/55, regular wall balls

Leg burner and lung burner for sure today.  The snatch is something you should be able to confidently cycle and I would like you to try to stick with this theme if possible throughout the workout.  Go to singles only as a last resort.  Otherwise, see if you can work some touch and go reps today and see how it goes.  That 2 for 1 means that you will have to do a squat while the wall ball is up in the air hitting the wall.  Speed is your friend here and you will most likely be needing to break these up due to the combo.  Modify with a lighter barbell and ball but if possible try to keep the 2 for 1 there instead of a traditional wall ball.

60 T2B
Goal: Drop no more than 6 times from the bar
Notes: Each time you drop from the bar do 30 Double Unders
Red - 40 T2B
White - 4 rds: 5 kip swings + 5 kip knee tucks, 30 singles after each round

High rep T2B today with a penalty each time you drop. Goal should be no more than 6 sets of double unders but try not to game things too much here.  You should be smart but also remind yourself this is a training day and breaking up the sets differently or trying bigger chunks can be a good learning lesson.  Back off on the reps if needed in order to keep the doubles reasonable.
