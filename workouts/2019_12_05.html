Metcon (AMRAP - Rounds and Reps)
Teams of 2: 30 min AMRAP
1 mile run
50 alt hang db snatches
30 db box step overs
10 rope climbs (no jump)
switch out every 200 m on the run, split reps on everything else as needed

* sub for rope climbs: 1 rope climb = 3 pull ups, 4 knee tucks, 5 air squats
