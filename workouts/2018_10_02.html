400m run
Goal: Sub 17 min
Red - 15/12/9 for Fran Reps
White - EB 12/9/6 for Fran Reps

This one is all about your engine.  Weight should be light enough that the thought of doing the thrusters unbroken is not an issue at the start.  It’s the willingness to just "go there" after the runs and with the volume.  If you need to scale things back to meet the time domain try doing 15-12-9 on the Fran portions but keep the runs there.  Push hard on the runs and trust that fitness on this one!
Metcon (No Measure)
3 Rounds
100' Handstand Walk
25 Cal Row
Goal: Consistency in HS Walk
Red - 50' HSW/20 shoulder taps/5 HSW to wall
White - Handstand hold :20/50', 15 Cal Row

This is more about the consistency with the handstand walk than it is on the time.  Push on the row and work on keeping your midline dialed in with the handstand walk while breathing hard.  See if you can keep the hs walks done in 25’ increments.  If you are still working on hs walks try a handstand hold against the wall for roughly :20 sec/50’ increment. If you want to add in some shoulders taps there as well that can be a good option.
