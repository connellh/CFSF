Metcon (Time)
For Time:
150 KB Swing (53/35)
75 HSPU
25 Ring Muscle Up
Goal: Sub 14 min/ 20 min cap
Red - 100 KBS/50 HSPU(up to 2 mats or Push Press 75/55)/25 banded or jumping ring MU's
White - 100 RKBS w/lighter KB, 50 push press (45/35), 25 banded strict pull ups

Higher rep work on this one so pacing is much more of a role on this workout.  You should be managing your work/rest cycles so your breaks are minimal and you don’t go to muscular failure.  If you need to scale back try to keep the higher reps and scale the weight on the swing.  For the HSPU and MU, do the movement if you have them but scale the reps.  Otherwise try a different option than you have done in the past.  Possibly banded, jumping, or a progression you find challenging for you and keep the reps a bit higher.
